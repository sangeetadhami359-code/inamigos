Our Journey

Compassion in Action Since 2020

InAmigos Foundation, founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating lasting social impact.

With its base in Chhattisgarh, the foundation operates across India, addressing critical societal issues through a network of dedicated professionals and volunteers.

At InAmigos Foundation, we believe in the power of collective action. Our transparent operations and strong partnerships enable us to bring real, measurable change to society. Through our social media presence and online platforms, we ensure that every initiative reaches the right audience, encouraging more individuals and organizations to join our mission.

Compliance & Trust

Credentials & Recognitions

We strictly maintain highest operational standards, legal compliances, and transparency audits.

Section 8 Registered

Licensed by the Central Government of India, operating as a professional non-profit organization.

80G & 12A Certified

Ensuring deep transparency, strict accountability, and 50% tax exemption benefits for donors.

CSR-1 Registered

Eligible to receive corporate funding and run structured Corporate Social Responsibility projects.

NITI Aayog Registered

Registered under the Darpan Portal, aligning our grassroots work with national development goals.

IAF ISO 9001:2015

Committed to maintaining high-quality management system standards across all operations.

Real Numbers

Our Initiatives & Impact

Key programs addressing critical needs and delivering measurable results over the last four years.

Project SEVA

Over 50,000+ meals and clothing distributed.

Distributing regular nutritional packets, hot meals, and fresh clothes to homeless and underprivileged families in rural and suburban belts.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Bridging education gaps & life-skills training.

Providing informal education, basic digital literacy, personal hygiene awareness, and essential learning materials to children in marginalized areas.

Project JEEV

Feeding 50+ stray animals daily.

Dedicated to animal safety and protection. Runs daily feed drives, provides emergency veterinary aid, and arranges shelters for street strays.

Project UDAAN

Empowering women through skill development.

Collaborating with rural self-help groups (SHGs) to promote financial independence, vocational training (like sewing/handicrafts), and menstrual health awareness.

Project PRAKRITI

Over 20,000+ saplings planted.

Supporting environmental conservation. Organizing regular tree plantation, eco-agriculture workshops, and local garbage clean-up campaigns.

Project VIKAS

Trained 30,000+ interns globally.

Facilitating employment opportunities through digital skill internships in content writing, digital marketing, data operations, research, and finance.

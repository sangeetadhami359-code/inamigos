How We Help

Initiatives Demanding Your Action

We target critical developmental and conservation needs. Choose a cause you believe in and help us create sustainable progress.

Project Seva

Food & Clothing Distribution

Targeted feeding drives supplying freshly prepared warm meals and seasonal clothing distributions to homeless populations and street kids in Chhattisgarh and surrounding regions.

Goal: ₹5,00,000 82% Funded
Support Project Seva
Project Bachpanshala

Education for Underprivileged

Bridging gaps in basic learning. We set up neighborhood digital classrooms, teach life skills, and sponsor schooling costs (fees, textbooks, and bags) for deserving kids.

Goal: ₹6,00,000 95% Funded
Support Bachpanshala
Project Jeev

Animal Rescue & Welfare

Dedicated to stray animal protection. Our local networks provide daily food bowls, rescue injured dogs/cats, distribute warm winter bedding, and organize adoption drives.

Goal: ₹3,00,000 70% Funded
Support Project Jeev
Project Udaan

Women Empowerment

Fostering financial independence. We run tailoring, stitching, and handicraft training centers for rural self-help groups (SHGs), alongside distribution of sanitary hygiene kits.

Goal: ₹4,00,000 65% Funded
Support Project Udaan
Project Prakriti

Environmental Conservation

Combatting climate risks. Focuses on organizing local seed planting drives, promoting eco-friendly composting models, and cleaning water channels to maintain natural balances.

Goal: ₹2,50,000 88% Funded
Support Project Prakriti
Project Vikas

Employability & Internships

Enhancing vocational skills. Guiding students through structured digital internships across marketing, finance, content, and data analytics to prep them for careers.

Goal: ₹8,00,000 92% Funded
Support Project Vikas

Why Donate to InAmigos?

Every small contribution adds up to drive massive social and environmental campaigns. Your funding helps buy food packets, print study worksheets, fund animal veterinary operations, buy plant seeds, and sponsor technical courses for youth.

80G Tax Deductions

As a certified non-profit organization, all donations made to InAmigos Foundation are eligible for 50% tax deductions under Section 80G of the Indian Income Tax Act. We provide official receipts immediately.

Secure & Audited

Our operations are regularly audited under Section 8, NITI Aayog, and ISO 9001:2015 certifications, ensuring complete accountability.

Corporate CSR Alliances

Are you a corporate representative looking for CSR project execution? InAmigos is CSR-1 registered under Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA). Reach out via our Contact Form to co-develop campaigns.

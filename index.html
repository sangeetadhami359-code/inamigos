About the Foundation

Get to Know Us Better

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government, based in Chhattisgarh, India.

We hold 80G & 12A certifications ensuring full transparency, accountability, and tax-exempt benefits for our donors. We are also CSR-1 registered, NITI Aayog registered, and hold the prestigious IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification for high quality standards.
